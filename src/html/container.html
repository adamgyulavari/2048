How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one! Press backspace to undo and full stop to accept the suggested move.

Note: This site is not the official version of 2048, but you can play the official version on your phone via http://git.io/2048. This site is a derivative or fake, and should be used with caution ;-).
